Strategy Module Library

Select the strategy modules that will drive portfolio construction. Each module applies a systematic rule to security selection within your Blueprint's asset allocation.

📈

Momentum

12-month trailing momentum with 1-month skip. Rank securities by performance, hold top quartile.

💎

Value

Price-to-book and earnings yield composite. Targets undervalued securities with fundamental support.

🛡

Low Volatility

Minimum variance construction within asset class. Reduces drawdown at the cost of some upside capture.

📊

Quality

Return on equity, debt-to-equity, and earnings stability composite. Tilts toward durable businesses.

⚖️

Equal Weight

Naive diversification baseline. Equal-weight within each asset class module. Low complexity, low cost.

🌐

Global Macro

Top-down regime detection. Shifts allocation between growth and defensive assets based on macro signals.
